Procedure International Journal of Science and Technology (PIJST) is the leading high quality International open access, peer-reviewed and refereed, multidisciplinary, Multilanguage and monthly published research journal, published by Institute of science and Technology Development (ISTD) with a commitment to promote academic research and innovation in all areas of science and technology as well as provides a platform to share the research findings of researchers, scholars and scientists with the global scientific community. PIJST is providing a platform to researchers, academicians, professional, practitioners and students to impart knowledge by publishing high quality empirical and theoretical research papers/articles, survey paper, review paper, Data Articles, Clinical Practice Articles, research proposal, case studies, Literature reviews and book reviews. The multidisciplinary editorial board mentioned in this journal reflects the diversity of subjects covered under the purview of science and technology. Under the realm of Science and Technology, the coverage includes Physical Science, Chemical Science, Mathematical Science, Life Science, Biological Science, Pharmaceutical Science, Medical Science, Ayurvedic Science, Engineering and Technology and Environmental Science to name a few. This diverse scope will play an important role in collaborating with researchers from different disciplines of science and technology and developing collective understanding in the above disciplines. PIJST will support the open access policy to make the fundamental principles and practical facts of science and technology research accessible to all. The peer reviewed process of manuscripts published in this journal involves expert reviewers who assess the originality, validity and importance of the manuscripts before they are published.
