Procedure International Journal of Science and Technology (PIJST) is the leading high quality International open access, peer-reviewed and refereed, multidisciplinary, Multilanguage and monthly published research journal. PIJST aim to explore various fields of research study such as applied, theoretical and experimental technical studies so as to exchange research information among scholars, researchers, engineers, physicians and scientists interested in the field of science and technology.
